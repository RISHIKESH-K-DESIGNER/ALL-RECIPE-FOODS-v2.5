Oreo Shake

Tasty Recipe

6 chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo)
3 scoops vanilla ice cream
¾ cup milk

Blend chocolate sandwich cookies, ice cream, and milk together in a blender until smooth. Pour into 2 glasses. and Design Your Cup Tasty and Smoothy shake is ready to serve

strawberry Thick Shake

Tasty Recipe

1/3 cup milk
1-1/2 cups vanilla ice cream
1/2 cup frozen unsweetened strawberries
1 tablespoon strawberry preserves

In a blender, combine all ingredients; cover and process until smooth. Pour into chilled glasses; serve immediately.

5 egg roll wrappers
10 sticks string cheese
water oil, for frying tommato sauce or your favorite Sauce

Slice the egg roll wrappers in half. Place a stick of string cheese on the edge of the egg roll wrapper, then fold the sides of the wrapper over the ends of the cheese. Wet the other edge of the wrapper with water, then roll it up tightly, pressing the edge to make sure it’s sealed. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and cheese. Heat oil in a pot. Fry the sticks until golden brown, then drain on a paper towel. Serve with sauce.
